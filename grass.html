Install Logic

It's the drainage, not the grass.

Bare
Grass

A balcony is not a ground-level lawn.

Water needs somewhere to go, or grass will host mould within a season. We install on a draining sub-base with weep points, cut the turf to scribe corners, and fix with exterior-grade adhesive — not tape.

  • 01Draining sub-base, not flat foam
  • 02UV-stable fibres, 8-year colour warranty
  • 03Scribed to walls and drains, no gaps
  • 04Lead-free, IS-certified for children's contact
