Process

Sub-floor is a religion here.

Oak

Most floor failures are sub-floor failures.

Before a plank is laid, we level the substrate to ±2mm across three metres, test moisture at three points, and apply acoustic underlay calibrated for your construction. It's why our floors don't creak in year six.

  • 01Moisture-tested sub-floor at three points
  • 02Self-levelling screed to ±2mm
  • 03Acoustic underlay, not the cheap foam
  • 04Scribed to walls, not caulked over
