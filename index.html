An atelier philosophy

We believe a room is never finished by furniture alone — it is quieted, warmed, and made whole by its surfaces.

For fourteen years, our family-run studio in Dharampeth has worked with architects, interior designers, and homeowners to source, specify, and install surfaces that feel tailored — not catalogued. We measure twice, cut once, and leave your home cleaner than we found it.
